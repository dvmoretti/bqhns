Dear Holy Name Member,

Our 94th Annual Communion Breakfast will take place on Sunday, March 9, 2014. We will begin with the celebration of mass with Biship DiMarzio, and the Department Chaplains. After Mass, we will march to Antun's Catering Hall.

Please scroll down and review the Breakfast Poster for specifics (honorees, price etc.). However, there are two very important items that I must bring to your attention. First, all members attending are expected to be in uniform. Second, we implore you to get your breakfast money in as early as possible. We would appreciate as many returns at the February 26th meeting as possible. We thank you for your continued support.

Please be advised that Saturday night the 8th is daylight savings time. Please be sure to move the clocks back an hour that night.
