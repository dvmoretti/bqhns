Holy Name Society Golf Sponsorship

We are always looking for sponsorship at our Annual Golf Outing. By raising money through sponsorship, we are able to maintain our membership dues, which are still only $10 for active and for retired. These prices are the lowest of all the organizations. In addition, it allows us to maintain a viable scholarship fund. It only costs $100 per hole. Please fill out the sponsorship form and send it to the following address:

Brooklyn Queens Holy Name Society
P.O. Box 260304
Bellerose, NY 11426

A beautiful placard with whatever you want to appear on it will be given to you after the outing.

Holy Name Society Golfers

We cordially invite any new golfers to join us at the outing. All the information is on the flyer. If you are interested, get your money in quickly. We sell out early!
