1 Day Nordic Lobster Fest

Date: Saturday, October 23, 2010

Cost: $140 per person

Itinerary

1100 - 1200: Light lunch at the Immaculate Conception Center - 7200 Douglaston Parkway

1200: Depart Immaculate Conception Center for Rhode Island

1530 - 1800: Lobster Fest (all you can eat) Includes: lobster, shrimp, steamers, potatoes, corn on the cob, filet mignon, prime rib, 2 dessert bars, etc. (see menu)

1800: Depart for Mohegan Sun

1900: Arrive at Mohegan Sun

2300: Depart Mohegan Sun

Price Includes

  • Light Lunch at Immaculate Conception Center
  • The Lobster Fest (taxes & gratuities)
  • Bonus at Mohegan Sun
  • Beverages on the bus

***** We will only be taking 48 people - first come, first served *****
