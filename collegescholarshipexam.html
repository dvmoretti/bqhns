College Scholarship

  • Starting with 2014-2015 College Scholarships, the Police Reserve Association will no longer be providing an exam to determine our winners.

  • Commencing this year, we will be using the applicants SAT scores.

  • To be eligible for a scholarship, the following criteria must be met. The applicant must be a senior in high school who will attend a two or four year college and matriculating between September 2105 and August of 2016.

  • The sponsor must be an active or retired Uniformed Member of the Service with three consecutive years of membership in the Holy Name Society.

  • An application along with an official copy of SAT scores must be post marked no later than Monday, June 1, 2015 and mailed to:

NYPD Brooklyn-Queens Holy name Society
P.O. Box 260304
Bellerose, NY 11426

  • There will be three awards presented at our June 24, 2015 cook-out meeting at Monsignor Romano's parish. The first place award will be for $1000 which is generously donated by Commissioner Reginald Ward, Chairman of The New York Law Enforcement Foundation. There will be two additional awards of $500 each presented that night.
